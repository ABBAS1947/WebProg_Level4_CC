Resident Evil 2:Remake

Plot

Resident Evil 2 Remake is a survival horror game that reimagines the classic 1998 game. Players step into the shoes of Leon S. Kennedy and Claire Redfield as they attempt to escape a zombie-infested Raccoon City following a viral outbreak.

Information

  • Release Date: January 25, 2019
  • Developer: Capcom
  • Publisher: Capcom
  • Platforms: PlayStation 4, Xbox One, PC
  • Genre: Survival Horror

Cast

  • Leon S. Kennedy - Nick Apostolides
  • Claire Redfield - Stephanie Panisello
  • Director: Kazunori Kadoi
  • Producer: Yoshiaki Hirabayashi

Characters

  • Leon S. Kennedy - A rookie police officer caught in the outbreak.
  • Claire Redfield - A college student searching for her brother, Chris.
  • Ada Wong - A mysterious woman with her own agenda.
  • William Birkin - A scientist responsible for the G-virus outbreak.

Reviews

Resident Evil 2 Remake has been praised for its stunning visuals, tense atmosphere, and faithful recreation of the original game's story. IGN rated it 9/10, calling it "a terrifying return to Raccoon City."
