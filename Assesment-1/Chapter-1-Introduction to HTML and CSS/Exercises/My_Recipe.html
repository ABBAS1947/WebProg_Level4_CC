Chicken Karahi

Chicken Karahi is a traditional Pakistani Punjabi dish made with tender chicken pieces cooked in a rich, spicy tomato-based gravy. It is typically enjoyed with naan or chapati.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (bone-in pieces)
  • 3 medium tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 onions (finely sliced)
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 3-4 green chilies (sliced)
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (chopped, for garnish)
  • 4 tablespoons oil

How to Actualy cook

  1. Heat oil in a karahi or deep pan and add cumin seeds. Let them crackle.
  2. Add onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Mix in ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add chicken pieces and fry until they turn white.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes, red chili powder, turmeric, and salt. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
  6. Cover and let the chicken cook on medium heat until tender.
  7. Once oil separates, add green chilies and cook for another 3-4 minutes uncovered.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  9. Serve hot with naan or chapati.
